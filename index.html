Smarter Classrooms: Why IoT is More Than Just Automation

I recently had the opportunity to talk about IoT for classrooms, not just in terms of how it works but why it makes a difference.

How IoT Works (Quick Recap)

  • Sensors collect real-time data.
  • The data is sent to the cloud for processing.
  • Machine Learning (ML) algorithms analyze the data.
  • Insights are displayed on a dashboard for decision-making.

Why Does This Matter?

In our classroom, only 2 out of 4 tube lights are turned on. Turning on the remaining two creates glare on the board, making it difficult to read.

How Can IoT Help?

  • Sensors can detect light intensity and reflection patterns.
  • Data is sent to the cloud and analyzed.
  • The system suggests optimal lighting placement for new classrooms.
